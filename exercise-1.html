Do you love to learn?

For last 1 year I'm teaching drawing for the school and college students for free. One of my biggest inspiration is Amir Khan sir's role in the film "Taare Zameen Par". And I mainly concentrate on teaching the various drawing fields like:

  • Free Hand Sketching
  • Pencil Drawing
  • Water Colour Painting
  • Cartoon Drawing for children

The major learning path for beginers in the field of drawing consists of the following steps given in below:

  1. Learning free hand pencil sketching and learning about different types of drawing pencils.
  2. Learning pencil drawing and its various stroke styles.
  3. Moving to Water Colour Painting
  4. Colourful Landscape drawing using watercolour.
  5. Learning to draw Portrait.
